01

Yuyintang Music Park 育音堂音乐公园

Livehouse
📍 Address
B1-02, No. 1398 Yuyuan Rd, Changning District, Shanghai (basement level 1)
🏷 Type
Indie music livehouse

Founded in 2004, Yuyintang is one of Shanghai's earliest livehouses, promoting live music and nurturing local indie bands for two decades. CNN once wrote: "New York has the legendary CBGB; Shanghai has its own Yuyintang." The Music Park branch sits in the basement of Yuyuan Road and is one of the main homes of Yese Te Tiao's long-running series LIVE IDOL LIFE SH.

02

YUYINTOWN 育音堂小镇

Livehouse · music town
📍 Address
Basement level 1 (LG-21), No. 658 Zhaohua Rd, Changning District, Shanghai
🚇 Transit
Metro Line 3/4 Yan'an West Rd station, Exit 1 — ~300 m / 5 min walk
🅿 Parking
Blue Zone D, B3 level

A 2024 new venue inside the underground space of C·PARK Haisu Cultural Plaza. After twenty years on the old Kaixuan Road site (closed 2024.07.01 due to city planning), Yuyintang relocated here to build "Yuyin Town," a youth music town with multiple halls (Hall C among them), alongside clubs like Specters. Yese Te Tiao's Bandset Oneman "Mingye" was held here.

03

World Tree Theater 世界树剧场

Idol theater
📍 Address
7F, Hall C, No. 800 East Nanjing Rd, Huangpu District, Shanghai (No.1 Department Store)
🏷 Type
Idol-culture offline venue

A venue centered on idol culture, blending song, dance, performance, livestreaming and interaction. On the 7th floor of the No.1 Department Store on East Nanjing Road, it is a core stronghold of Shanghai's underground-idol scene — on nights like Valentine's Day it hosts six-hour underground-idol showcases, with an audience largely of camera-wielding twenty- and thirty-somethings.

04

Future Theater 未来剧场

Theater
📍 Address
2F, No. 179 Yichang Rd, Putuo District, Shanghai (Bandai Namco Shanghai Cultural Center)
🏷 Type
Small-to-mid theater

Formerly "Qianshuiwan Q-HOUSE small theater," later renamed, under the Bandai Namco Shanghai Cultural Center. A regular stage for underground-idol shows — where Yese Te Tiao revealed new member Youli and original #6 "Wicked Night."

05

7HERTZ LIVE

Livehouse
📍 Address
Building 4, No. 731 Hongxu Rd, Minhang District, Shanghai (Xijiao Creative Industry Park)
🎫 Tickets
Showstart (秀动)

A live-performance space inside the Xijiao Creative Industry Park, hosting underground-idol shows, KPOP parties, JAM Shanghai POP and other diverse events — one of western Shanghai's key idol / pop-music venues.

06

Singer Space 新歌空间

New performance space
📍 Address
3F, No. 1250 Yuyuan Rd, Changning District, Shanghai (Changning Workers' Cultural Palace)
☎ Phone
021-52378662

Opened in 2023, operated by Yuemin Culture and the Yuyintang team, hosting outstanding plays, original music shows and high-quality public events. Equipped for small music performances, rehearsals, recording and livestreaming; selected as one of Shanghai's 100 "New Performance Spaces."
